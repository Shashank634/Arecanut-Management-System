About Arecanut

Arecanut, also known as betel nut, is the seed of the Areca palm. It plays a significant role in Indian culture and economy. Widely used in rituals and traditional chewing practices, it is an important cash crop in southern India, especially Karnataka.

Arecanuts are categorized by their processing methods, such as boiled (red supari) or dried (white supari). Deepa Traders specializes in sourcing the finest quality arecanuts directly from farmers and ensuring proper grading, packaging, and timely delivery.

We support sustainable farming and ensure our products meet both domestic and international market standards. Our goal is to educate buyers and farmers alike about the importance of quality, hygiene, and transparency in trade.
