Welcome Deepa Traders

Deepa Traders is a family-owned business established in 1995, specializing in high-quality arecanut trading. With over 25 years of experience in the industry, we have built a reputation for reliability, quality, and fair pricing.

Our mission is to connect farmers with markets, ensuring fair prices for producers while delivering premium quality arecanut to our customers. We work directly with farmers across the region, providing guidance on cultivation practices and offering competitive prices for their produce.

At Deepa Traders, we believe in sustainable trading practices that benefit all stakeholders in the arecanut supply chain. Our team is committed to maintaining the highest standards of quality control and customer service.

Our Works

Arecanut Pealing Manual

At Deepa Traders, arecanut processing is a meticulous craft. We use traditional cutting methods that ensure each nut is handled with care to preserve its natural flavor and quality. Our skilled workers clean, cut, and sort arecanuts to maintain uniform size and appearance.

By combining heritage techniques with modern hygiene practices, we ensure that the end product is premium-grade and safe for consumption. The cutting process is carried out in a dust-free environment with strict quality checks at every stage.


Arecanut Pealing Machine

Areca peeling machine is developed specifically for the setting angle between the two cutter blades, which creates a shear force in the incision of dried areca nuts so that it can improve the quality of the peeling results.

An areca peeler, also known as a dehusker or husking machine, is a device used to remove the outer husk from areca nuts, which are the seeds of the Areca catechu palm. This process is typically done manually with knives, but machines can be used to automate and expedite the process, especially for large-scale processing. Areca nut peeling machines come in various forms, including manual, pedal-operated, and motorized versions

Our Arecanut Varieties

Red Arecanut

Premium quality red arecanut with high tannin content. Known for its rich color and superior taste. Ideal for traditional consumption and religious ceremonies.

White Arecanut

Carefully processed white arecanut with mild flavor. Popular in pan masala and chewing tobacco industries. Processed using traditional sun-drying methods.

Orange Arecanut

Orange arecanut refers to the color and shape of the fruit that bears the areca nut, not a specific variety of areca nut itself. The areca nut, also known as betel nut, is found inside a hard, orange-colored fruit.
