About Arecanut

Arecanut, also known as betel nut, is a key commercial crop in India. Grown primarily in southern states like Karnataka, it plays a major role in supporting rural economies.

At Deepa Traders, we are committed to empowering arecanut farmers through high-quality inputs, transparent pricing, and expert guidance. Arecanut is commonly used in religious rituals, Ayurvedic medicine, and as a chewable stimulant with betel leaves.

Our team works closely with growers to ensure healthy crops, sustainable practices, and a strong supply chain that supports both tradition and innovation in agriculture.
